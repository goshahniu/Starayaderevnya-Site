2025 Garden window escape

Gosha Hniu: cries and whispers, wheel lyre, marching band kazoo, percussion and objects

Maya Pik: synthesizer, flute, drum machine

Ran Nahmias: silent cello, santur4,6, oud1, voice3

Grundik Kasyansky: feedback synthesizer

Miguel Pérez: guitars

Design by Maya Pik

Recording, mix, mastering and production by Gosha Hniu

Mastering for vinyl by Victor Levin
Recorded on 11th August 2022 in Bonafide Studios, London. Additional recordings 2023-2024 in Israel, UK, Mexico and Bulgaria. Released by Ramble Records and Auris Media on 2nd May 2025.
