Skull Mask is a solo guitar project by Miguel Pérez. Since Miguel has joined the Staraya derevnya collective in 2021, Skull Mask began to perform as a duo with Gosha Hniu on the wheel lyre. Together we released "iká" in 2022, and a new album is set for 2025. Full Skull Mask discography is available on discogs.
2023 Iká
Miguel Pérez: guitar
Gosha Hniu: wheel lyre

Iká 1 was recorded on 10 August 2022 at Café Oto, London by Billy Steiger. It was mixed and mastered by Hniu.

Iká 2 was recorded and mixed on 13 August 2022 at Supernormal Festival by James King and mastered by Hniu.

Design and artwork by Maya Pik.
