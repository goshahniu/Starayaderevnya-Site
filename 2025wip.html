2025 Garden window escape

Gosha Hniu: cries and whispers, wheel lyre, marching band kazoo, percussion and objects / Maya Pik: synthesizer, flute, drum machine / Ran Nahmias: silent cello, santur4,6, oud1, voice3 / Grundik Kasyansky: feedback synthesizer / Miguel Pérez: guitars / Yoni Silver: bass clarinet / Andrea Serafino: drums1,3,4.

Recorded on 11 Aug. 2022 in Bonafide Studios, London. Additional recordings 2023-2024 in Israel, UK, Mexico and Bulgaria.

Artwork by Danil Gertman. Design by Maya Pik. Lyrics are based on the poems by Arthur Molev. Recording, mix, mastering and production by Gosha Hniu. Mastering for vinyl by Victor Levin.


Ramble Records & Auris Media 2025.
