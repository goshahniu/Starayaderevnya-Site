2022 Boulder blues
Gosha Hniu: objects, percussion, cries and whispers, marching band kazoo, wheel lyre1,2,4 / Ran Nahmias: silent cello, theremin / Maya Pik: synthesizer, flute4,5 / Grundik Kasyansky: feedback synthesizer / Miguel Pérez: guitars / Yoni Silver: bass clarinet / Andrea Serafino: drums / Tom Wheatley: double bass / Galya Chikiss: cries and whispers3 / Dasha and Masha Gerzon: choir1, piano4.

Recorded throughout 2020-2022 in Israel, UK, Mexico and Germany. "Bubbling pelt" originally performed at Tusk Festival 2020.

Artwork by Danil Gertman. Design by Maya Pik. Lyrics are based on the poems by Arthur Molev.
Recording, mix, mastering and production by Gosha Hniu.
